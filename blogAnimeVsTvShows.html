Anime Vs TV Shows

September 2021

Which is better Anime or TV Shows?

As some of you might not know what anime is or might still think of it as kids cartoon show lets see what it really is

What is Anime?

So first of all let me make it clear to you that anime is not a kids cartoon show or some "animated" TV show.

Anime is unique and innovative style of storytelling originated in Japan

In animes characters aren't bound by human limits and story doesn't have to be constrained by the thought of human limitation.
Its simply represent the different spectrum of imagination and unique way of presenting it.

How Anime is different ?

  • In general most of the animation will have one unique concept of power source for eg.
    • - Devil Fruit in One piece.
    • - Nen ability in Hunter x Hunter
    • - Alchemy in Full Metal Alchemist
    • - Chakra in Naruto
    • - Cursed energy in jujutsu kaise
    • - ki or chi in Dragon Ball

    and it goes on like this.

  • Action will be Limitless - It doesn't need to be bounded by laws of physics
  • Diversity of Character Class.
  • creativity at highest level - Each anime is unique in some form and represents writers creativity of thoughts

Which is better ?

In my opinion anime is way ahead of general TV Shows, It has better and unique way of storytelling. Stories can be as good as writers imagination without any boundation. Its powerpacked with emotions, action and comedy.

  • It has unbeatable comedy
  • Astonishing visuals
  • They talk about serious life lessons
  • It represents why friendship should be valued and treasured.


At the end i'll recommend you to watch at least one anime series in your life and then decide for yourself which one is better.
